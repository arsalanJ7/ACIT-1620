Tamanawis Athletics

The Tamanawis Athletic department is dedicated to give every student a chance to participate in the Athletic Program. Tamanawis secondary veiws sports as an essential component of its athletic program and seeks to include a sense of pride, devotion, and sportsmanship in it students. All kids should have access to oppourtunities that foster a meaningfull learning enviroment.

Club Philosophy

At Tamanawis we aim to strive a player centred enviroment to shape, create, and empower the youth to success. We also take high regard of respect, lifestyle, and excellence.
